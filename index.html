Harapan Baru (New Hope).

A doorway into the lives of women whose stories embody courage, compassion, and endurance. In the face of conflict, disaster, and hardship, they rose as leaders, storytellers, and guardians of peace.

UN Women presents this collection to honor their experiences and to inspire us all to recognize that peace is not an ideal we hope for, but a journey we must walk together.

The Women, Peace and Security (WPS) agenda is built on four main pillars:

  • Protection: Safeguarding the rights and safety of women and girls during conflict and crises.
  • Prevention: Working to stop violence and conflict before it happens, including addressing risks of gender-based violence.
  • Participation: Ensuring women have a real voice and role in peace talks, decision-making, and community efforts.
  • Relief & Recovery: Supporting women, families, and communities to heal and rebuild after conflict or disaster.

Together, these pillars highlight that lasting peace is only possible when women's experiences and leadership are fully recognized and included.
